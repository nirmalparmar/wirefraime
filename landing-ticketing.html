FAQ

Questions, answered.

Seatr charges organizers zero platform fees on all plans. We earn revenue through optional premium plan subscriptions. You pay only the standard payment-processor fee (typically 2.9% + $0.30/transaction), which we pass through at cost. No hidden fees, no revenue share.
Revenue from ticket sales is deposited into your linked bank account on a rolling daily basis — typically within 1 business day of each sale. You don't have to wait until after your event. Most organizers see their first deposit within 24–48 hours of their first sale.
Absolutely. Free events are completely free to process — no payment fees, no platform fees. Use Seatr for registration, check-in, and attendee management at zero cost.
You control your refund policy. Issue full or partial refunds to any attendee directly from your dashboard with one click. Seatr handles all communication and reverse payouts automatically. Our platform fee stays $0 regardless.
You decide. You can absorb the processing fee yourself or pass it on to the buyer — our checkout handles both transparently. Many organizers advertise "no booking fees" and bake the processing cost into the ticket price.
